Help & Instructions

What every part of the app does, and how to use it. Tap a section to expand.

Core & tracking
🏠Main

Your daily goals list, rolling over at 6 AM each day (not midnight) so a late night doesn't reset "today" early. Goals you add for tomorrow carry over automatically.

💪Fitness

Tracks lifts by training day (Push/Pull/Legs by default, fully customizable in its own Settings). Logs your top set per exercise and tells you when to add weight based on your rep target.

  • 1RM calculator — tap the calculator icon next to the gear; estimates your one-rep max from any weight/reps pair using the Epley formula, plus a training-percentage table.
  • Import strength plan — photo, library image, or pasted text; AI reads it and merges exercises into your existing days by name match.
  • Weight tracking + InBody scans — log body weight, or photograph an InBody body-composition printout for full metrics (body fat %, muscle mass, segmental balance).
  • Nova (the chat icon) — an AI coach with context on your whole training history.
💊Health

Supplement stack tracker (daily check-offs, low-stock awareness) and the embedded Water tracker. WHOOP recovery/sleep/resting-heart-rate connects here.

Setup needed: WHOOP_CLIENT_ID / WHOOP_CLIENT_SECRET in Vercel, from a WHOOP developer app.
💧Water

Daily water target computed from your weight, activity level, sex, age, and any logged caffeine/substances (NAM/IOM formula: ~35ml per kg of body weight, plus adjustments). The number in the top-right pill on every page reflects this target.

☕Caffeine

Tracks intake against your recovery-adjusted daily limit (lower on poor-recovery WHOOP days) and shows a decay curve so you can time your last cup before it affects sleep.

📊Finance

Net worth, spending, subscriptions, and orders. Base currency is USD internally regardless of your display currency. Nova here is a money coach with your financial snapshot as context; the receipt scanner reads photos of receipts/bank screenshots and logs the figures automatically.

🏃Marathon

Full training-plan tracker: import a plan (photo/text), sync actual runs from Strava, track weekly mileage/pace/completion-rate against your goal pace, and get a per-day routine with fueling reminders pulled from your supplement stack.

  • Take a rest day button converts today into a completed rest day in one tap.
  • Hover any chart point or glossary term (GMP, tempo, etc.) for an explanation.
  • Checking off a day manually sticks — a later Strava sync won't silently flip it back.
Setup needed: STRAVA_CLIENT_ID / STRAVA_CLIENT_SECRET in Vercel, from a Strava API application.
🧠Nova Lite

A standalone AI mentor with visibility into everything saved across the dashboard. Has a mic button (speech-to-text) and a speaker toggle (spoken replies) — upgrades to ElevenLabs automatically if configured, otherwise uses the free browser voice.

Personal organization
📝Notes

Simple notes with search, pinning, and a full-screen editor. Empty notes are dropped automatically when you close them.

✅Chores

One-off or recurring (daily/weekly) tasks. Recurring items reset themselves automatically each new day/week — no cron job needed, it's computed from the date each time you open the page.

🧴Personal Care

Two halves: daily check-offs (contacts, shaving, moisturizer — anything you do every day) and supply tracking (shampoo, sunscreen — anything that runs out), which warns you when a product is about to run low based on how many days you said it typically lasts.

🔗Saved Links

Paste any URL (TikTok, YouTube, articles) to save it, organized by category you choose. On iPhone, sharing a link from another app to this one (via the share sheet) may save it directly — support varies by iOS version, so pasting is the reliable fallback.

📄Resume Review

Upload a resume as a PDF or photo — AI gives a 1-10 score, strengths, weaknesses, and concrete rewrite suggestions. PDFs are read with full visual understanding (tables, formatting), not just raw text extraction.

📅Calendar

The master calendar — aggregates Google Calendar, your marathon training schedule, Canvas assignments, and daily goals into one agenda grouped by day, 14 days out.

Setup needed: GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID / GOOGLE_CLIENT_SECRET in Vercel, from a Google Cloud OAuth client (Calendar API enabled).
Health & lifestyle
🥗Nutrition

Calorie and macro targets computed from your weight/height/age/sex/activity (Mifflin-St Jeor formula) with a cut/maintain/bulk goal toggle. Log food manually against the targets.

🧖Skincare

Build an AM/PM routine — each product gets a usage tip (wet vs dry skin, ordering, frequency) automatically. The "AI routine check" reviews your full combination for ordering mistakes or conflicting actives (e.g. retinol + vitamin C same night).

🛍️Shoppingno live links

Pick your style(s), get AI-suggested brands and specific pieces to search for yourself — the AI has no live shopping access, so it never invents a real product URL or claims current pricing/stock. Scan receipts to build a purchase log, which the AI uses to avoid suggesting near-duplicates.

College & career
🎓CollegeWIP

Pulls Canvas assignments, quizzes, and calendar events, grouped by course, filtered to what's not yet submitted. An AI button suggests the fastest order to clear everything, estimating effort per task since Canvas doesn't provide that.

Setup needed: a Canvas Personal Access Token — self-serve, no admin needed. On canvas.case.edu: Account → Settings → "+ New Access Token."
💼Internshipsmanual send

Add opportunities, paste your resume/bio once, and get an AI-drafted personalized email per opportunity. You review, edit, and send yourself (copy to clipboard or open your email client) — there's no automated sending, by design.

System
✨Your Coach

The floating button in the bottom-right corner of every page (except Finance, which has its own nav). Opens a chat with full visibility into every module's data. The first time you open it each browser session, it proactively scans everything and tells you the 1-3 most relevant things worth knowing — you don't have to ask. Has voice input/output like Nova Lite.

⚙️Settings

Gear icon on the homepage opens quick personal-data fields (height/weight/birthday/sex/activity — used across Water, Nutrition, Caffeine). The "Advanced settings" link there opens the full settings page: API spend analysis, model picker, conservation mode, budget alerts, themes, data import/export, and version history.

🔔Notifications

A daily reminder push notification. Only works once the app is added to your phone's home screen (iOS 16.4+). Enable from Settings → Notifications.

Setup needed: VAPID_PUBLIC_KEY / VAPID_PRIVATE_KEY / VAPID_SUBJECT in Vercel.
🔒Site login

The whole site is gated behind a password (separate from any module's own login). Sessions last 180 days once you sign in. If you ever need to log everyone out instantly, change SITE_PASSWORD in Vercel — it invalidates every existing session immediately.
